Biography

dEEGE begun his producer career at 2019. He decided to collaborate with his friend as both were just starting out. Since the beginning of his journey, he has explored different genres. Some of those genres are hip-hop, rnb, dnb, garage and techno. He is today actively exploring new genres in ways to expand his style.


dEEGE has studied 1 year at Karlskoga Folkhögskola in Music Production. Through his school year he got to collab with different producers and artists working in different genres. At Karlskoga Folkhögskola he has his first live performance on a stage. Mostly of dEEGE's producer career was shaped in his bedroom, that's where he has learnt the most and built his own universe.

For quite some time, music has been mostly a hobby and not a profession, though he does provide services on his free-time if there is payment.
